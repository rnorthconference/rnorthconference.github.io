Registration Scholarship Application (DUE 5 PM ON SUNDAY, AUGUST 1, 2021)

noRth is committed to celebrating and enhancing the diversity of the R community. With this in mind, we are offering full registration scholarships to underrepresented and historically marginalized groups in the R community. In order to apply, please describe:
  • Your personal journey as an R user, including how you are underrepresented or a part of a historically marginalized group in the R community
  • Your career goals (specifically or broadly, like what kinds of problems you want to solve) and how this conference will help you move closer to realizing your goals
Submit your 500 word or fewer response using the form below with the subject: Scholarship Application (Your Name). Please use the form to submit any other questions to the conference organizers.
